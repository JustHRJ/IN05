Products - Laser Welding Wires

We distribute and sell laser welding wires or micro welding wires or welding wires ranging from 0.1mm to 0.8mm. We carry and have stock a wide variety of welding filler materials and wires daily for our jobshop.

Our Laser Welding wires and Laser Micro Welding Fillers are specifically manufactured in USA, Europe and Japan, depending mostly on the welding application and the source of the workpiece material.

All laser welding wires and fillers are critically cleaned, and packaged for the highest quality welding filler materials available anywhere in the world. Every batch are individually monitored very closely for hardness and surface finishing and conforms to the specification of each and every base material.

Our USA laser welding micro wires are placed in inert plastic tubes and then the tubes are atmospherically purged with argon and then finally hermetically sealed, with HQA 2 year Barrier Protection System to protect and prolong the lifespan of your wires.
